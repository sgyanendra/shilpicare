Who We Are

ShilpiCare Foundation is a grassroots NGO dedicated to empowering women and protecting the environment. We began by creating job opportunities for local women and later expanded to tackle air pollution through biomass fuel solutions.

Our Mission

"To empower women through dignified employment and promote sustainable, eco-friendly practices that uplift communities and protect the environment."

Our Vision

A future where every woman is financially independent, every farmer earns with dignity, and every community thrives in harmony with nature.
