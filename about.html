Driving Change Through Grassroots Action

At ShilpiCare Foundation, we focus on initiatives that directly impact lives and the environment. From creating livelihood opportunities for women to launching clean-energy projects, every step we take is toward a more empowered and sustainable future.

1. Women Empowerment Through Factory Jobs

  • Skill Training: Stitching, packaging, machine operation, inventory handling, quality control.
  • Fair Employment: Equal wages, safe workspaces, balanced shifts.
  • Financial Literacy: Workshops on savings, banking, and digital payments.
  • Community Impact: Many women have become family breadwinners and role models.

“I never thought I could earn with my hands. Now I’m helping other women learn and grow too.”
— Sarita Devi, Factory Worker

2. Biomass Pellet Procurement from Farmers

North India faces dangerous levels of air pollution during winters due to stubble burning. We’re solving this through:

  • Purchasing crop waste from farmers to prevent burning
  • Converting into biomass pellets – a clean fuel
  • Powering factory units or collaborating with clean energy firms

Our Goals:

  • 👨‍🌾 Extra income for farmers
  • 🌬️ Reduce stubble-burning pollution
  • 🔄 Create a circular economy
  • 🧯 Use clean energy to power factories
